Ranged Double-Handed Fire Elementalist

Inspired by this Steam Thread, especially recreating the build of talonblade3.

Leveling Guide

  • Look for Francis' Gun in the Lower Crossing. Switch to a double handed ranged weapon as soon as possible.
  • Choose class
  • 1 point in bar (Demolitionist, 1/50)
  • 3 point into Fire Strike (3/12)
  • 4 points in bar (Demolitionist, 5/50)
  • 1 point into Flame Touched (1/12)
  • 1 point into Blackwater Cocktail (1/12)
  • 5 points in bar (Demolitionist, 10/50)
  • 1 point into Explosive Strike (1/12)
  • 1 point into Vindictive Flame (1/16)
  • 5 points in bar (Demolitionist, 15/50)
  • 1 points into Searing Might (1/3)
  • 5 point into Grenado (5/16)
  • 5 points in bar (Demolitionist, 20/50)
