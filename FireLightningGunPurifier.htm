Dual Wield Fire Lightning Gun Purifier

by requnix & AKDONG

Demolitionist + Inquisitor = Purifier

Faction of Choice: Kymon’s Chosen (but doesn't really matter)

Final Level 100 Stat Distribution: All in Physique

Gameplay

Have Fire Strike as your man attack and keep Word of Renewal up. Then, stand back, in your Inquisitor Seal, and damage enemies as they run towards you. As long as you do damage you also get healed. When you have to kite enemies, do it. Stronger enemies can be weakened using Thermite Mine.
