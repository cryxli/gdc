Leveling Guide

The following list shows a possible way to level a character up.

Phase 1

  • Choose class
  • 1 point into bar (Shaman 1/50)
  • 11 point into Devouring Swarm (11/16)
  • RESKILL: Remove all points from Devouring Swarm (0/16)
  • 9 point into bar (Shaman 10/50)
  • 16 point into Summon Briarthorn (16/16)
  • 6 point into Mogdrogen’s Pact (6/12)
  • 5 point into bar (Shaman 15/50)
  • 2 points into Heart of the Wild (2/10)
  • 5 points into bar (Shaman 20/50)
  • 3 points into Ground Slam (3/12)
  • 5 points into bar (Shaman 25/50)
  • 1 point into Oak Skin (1/10)
  • 1 point into Wendigo Totem (1/12)
  • 6 point into Mogdrogen’s Pact (12/12)

Phase 2

  • Choose class
  • 1 point into bar (Occultist 1/50)
  • 7 points into Curse of Frailty (7/10)
  • 14 point into bar (Occultist 15/50)
  • 5 points into Blood of Dreeg (5/16)
  • 1 points into Grasping Vines (1/12)
  • 1 points into Entangeling of Dreeg (1/16) ??? Entangling Vines (1/12) ???
  • 3 points into Heart of the Wild (2/10)
  • 3 points into Curse of Frailty (10/10)

Phase 3

  • 15 points into bar (Shaman 40/50)
  • 1 points into Blood Pact (1/12)
  • 1 points into Emboldening Presence (1/12)
  • 10 points into bar (Shaman 50/50)
  • 1 points into Conjure Primal Spirit (1/12)
  • 12 points into Primal Bond (12/12)
  • 17 point into bar (Occultist 32/50)
  • 1 points into Bonds of Bysmiel (1/12)
  • 7 points into Aspect of the Guardian (7/12)
